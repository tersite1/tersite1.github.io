# **About Me**

Hi, I am **{{ site.author.name }}** :wave:,

I am an M.S. student at KAIST in the [Computational Intelligence Laboratory](https://cilabs.kaist.ac.kr/) (Prof. Changick Kim). I earned a B.S. in Civil Engineering (AI Convergence) from Yonsei University, Seoul, and interned at Seoul National University in both the [Vehicle Intelligence Laboratory](https://vi.snu.ac.kr/) and the Autonomous Robot Intelligence Laboratory, working on robotics perception.

I am driven by **real-world applications and solving real problems**. I founded and exited [DepthViz](https://github.com/tersite1/DepthViz), an on-device iOS LiDAR SLAM app acquired by a construction firm, and worked as an AI Engineer at [Stellarvision](https://stellarvision.co.kr/en/), where I generated 3D digital twins from **drone-mounted SAR imagery** for landslide prevention — implemented with Neural Radiance Fields and exhibited at **CES 2025**. I hold **nine patents** in LiDAR mapping, 3D reconstruction, and UAV systems, and I am currently CTO of [IDOLL Robotics](https://idoll.love/), developing real-world robotic systems.

My research interests center on **autonomous robotic sensing, LiDAR-inertial SLAM, and as-built 3D modeling** for construction and infrastructure. My long-term dream is to become a professor who advances real-world robotic sensing and trains the next generation of scholars.
