## DepthViz — A LiDAR Scanner on iPhone

DepthViz is an on-device iOS LiDAR-inertial SLAM application that turns an iPhone into a real-time 3D scanner. I founded the project, shipped it to the App Store (peaking at **#36**), and the company was **acquired by a construction firm**. The project is open-sourced.
