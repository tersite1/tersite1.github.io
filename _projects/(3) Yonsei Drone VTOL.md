## Quad-Tiltrotor VTOL Drone for Autonomous Search and Rescue

A VTOL platform with an adjustable center of gravity, designed and flown for autonomous search-and-rescue missions. The work won **Joint 1st Prize at the 23rd AAM (Advanced Air Mobility) Tech Challenge, KASA**, and was published at KRoC 2026.
