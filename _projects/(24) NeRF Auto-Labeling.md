## NeRF-Assisted Auto-Labeling Pipeline

Undergraduate research at the Autonomous Robot Intelligence Lab (ARIL). I reconstruct objects from 2D images with Instant-NGP, render novel 2D views from the 3D model, auto-annotate the rendered views, and train a YOLO detector on the augmented data (for example, quadruped-robot detection).
