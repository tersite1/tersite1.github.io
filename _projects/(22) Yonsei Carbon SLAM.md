## Forest Carbon Estimation via LiDAR-Inertial SLAM

Graduation project (Best Project). I built an end-to-end LiDAR-inertial SLAM pipeline based on DLIO, calibrated a Livox Mid-70 LiDAR with an IMU, and reconstructed dense point clouds of a forest. From the reconstruction I counted individual trees with Euclidean clustering and estimated the forest's carbon sequestration.
